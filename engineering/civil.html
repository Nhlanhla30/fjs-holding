Civil & Building

From turnkey commercial and industrial builds to structural and civil works, pipelines, tanks and storage — delivered ground-up through a single accountable team.

FJS Holding's civil and building team delivers new construction, renovation and restoration, structural and civil infrastructure, and large-scale pipelines, tanks and storage — backed by in-house engineering and project-management capability across the commercial, industrial, water and energy sectors.

Commercial & Industrial Construction

  • New build across commercial, industrial and institutional sectors
  • Multi-storey, mixed-use and high-density developments
  • Residential estate and community housing
  • Government, healthcare and education facilities

Renovation & Restoration

  • Building upgrades and modernisation
  • Commercial and institutional renovation
  • Historic building restoration and conservation
  • Structural concrete repair and rehabilitation
  • Project management and client representation

Structural & Civil Works

  • Civil infrastructure — water structures, bridges and dams
  • Roads and bulk earthworks

Pipelines, Tanks & Storage

  • Bulk water supply and purification infrastructure
