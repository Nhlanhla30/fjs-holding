Good to Know

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers on the sectors and regions we serve, our compliance status and how to get a quote.

Which sectors does FJS Holding serve?
We work across energy and power, mining, water and wastewater, public infrastructure, and commercial and industrial development — delivering electrical, mechanical and civil scopes in each.
Which regions do you operate in?
We're based in Johannesburg, Gauteng and take on projects across South Africa. Recent work spans Gauteng, Limpopo and the Northern Cape, and we mobilise nationally for projects of the right scale.
Are you CIDB and BBBEE registered?
Yes. FJS Holding is CIDB registered across four classes of works — Civil Engineering (CE), Electrical: Building (EB), Electrical: Infrastructure (EP) and Mechanical (ME) — and a BBBEE Level 2 Contributor. We also hold a COIDA Letter of Good Standing and are fully SARS tax compliant — company registration number 2022/209029/07.
Do you handle multi-discipline or turnkey projects?
Yes — it's our core strength. With electrical, mechanical and civil teams in-house, we deliver integrated and turnkey projects through a single accountable partner, reducing coordination risk.
How do I request a quote?
Complete the enquiry form above, email sales@fjsholding.co.za, or call 061 424 3723. Sharing your project scope, location and timeline helps us route your enquiry to the right discipline lead quickly.
