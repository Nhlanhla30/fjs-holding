Engineering

Solar & Renewable Energy

From a single rooftop to a full-scale energy plant — engineered for maximum yield, grid compliance and long-term reliability.

FJS Holding designs, supplies and installs solar PV systems for residential, commercial and industrial clients across South Africa — from a single rooftop installation to a full-scale energy plant. Every system is engineered for maximum yield, grid compliance and long-term reliability.

System Types

Three ways to power your site

01

Grid-Tied

Offset your daytime consumption and reduce your bill, feeding from the grid when the sun is down. The simplest, fastest-payback option.

02

Off-Grid

Full independence for remote and rural sites — generation plus battery storage sized to carry the load without a utility connection.

03

Hybrid

The best of both — grid connection with battery backup for loadshedding resilience and the option to store or export surplus.

Who We Serve

Built for every scale

Residential

  • Standard rooftop installation, neatly mounted and cabled
  • Load-shedding protection and battery backup
  • System sizing matched to your household consumption
  • SSEG registration with your municipality

Commercial

  • Rooftop and carport arrays that work your roof space hard
  • Demand reduction to cut peak-tariff costs
  • Finance and PPA options to suit your cash flow

Industrial

  • Large-scale ground-mount and rooftop generation
  • Grid export and wheeling where permitted
  • Full plant delivery — design, build and commissioning
  • NERSA compliance and registration
Battery Storage & Backup

Power through loadshedding

We size battery storage to carry your essential — or entire — load through outages, with quality inverters and monitoring so you always know your state of charge.

  • Loadshedding resilience for homes, offices and plants
  • System sizing matched to your critical loads and budget
  • Quality inverters and supported battery brands
  • Remote monitoring of generation, storage and consumption
Compliance & Certification

Connected and compliant

Every grid-connected system is registered and certified — so your installation is legal, safe and insurable.

SSEG

Embedded Generation

Small-Scale Embedded Generation registration with your municipality for grid-tied and hybrid systems.

NERSA

Licensing

NERSA registration and licensing support for larger commercial and industrial installations.

CoC

Certificate on Completion

Electrical Certificate of Compliance issued on completion of every installation.
