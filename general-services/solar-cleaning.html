Solar Panel Cleaning & Maintenance

One of the highest-ROI maintenance activities for any solar installation — keeping your panels clean keeps your generation up.

Dirty panels can lose up to 30% of their output.

Dust, bird fouling and grime steadily reduce how much your array generates. Regular cleaning is one of the highest-ROI maintenance activities for any solar installation — and it pays for itself in recovered generation.
