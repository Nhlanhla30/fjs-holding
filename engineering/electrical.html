Engineering

Electrical Engineering

High-voltage power infrastructure, electro-mechanical systems, renewable installations and specialist data centre electrical — engineered, energised and maintained to utility standards.

FJS Holding's electrical division delivers high-voltage power infrastructure, electro-mechanical systems, renewable energy installations and specialist data centre electrical packages across South Africa's energy, mining and industrial sectors.

Power Infrastructure

  • HV, MV and LV installation and commissioning
  • High-voltage substation design and build
  • Overhead power line construction
  • Power transformer manufacture and supply
  • Switchgear design, manufacture and supply
  • Power reticulation networks
  • Community and urban electrification

Electro-Mechanical Equipment

  • Electric motors — standard and specialist variants
  • Industrial batteries, backup and DC power
  • Industrial turbo-machinery and rotating equipment
  • Specialist drive and signalling systems
  • Cable supply and installation
  • Lighting and energy-saving installations

Renewable Energy

  • Solar and wind energy plant construction
  • Full project delivery for wind and solar
  • Communications and data network installation
  • Electrical installations under energised conditions
  • Rural and remote renewable project delivery

Data Centre & Maintenance

  • MEP packages for data centres
  • Backup power and power-quality systems
  • Dedicated power station and substation construction
  • Planned and emergency plant shutdowns
  • Switchgear repair and network maintenance
  • Mining and heavy-industry electrical maintenance
Standards & Safety
