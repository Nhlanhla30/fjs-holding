Our Story

One partner, two divisions.

FJS Holding is a multi-division specialist operating across South Africa's engineering and general-services sectors. Established in 2022, we deliver end-to-end solutions — from initial scoping and procurement through to construction, commissioning and ongoing support.

Our work spans renewable energy installations, heavy industrial plant, civil infrastructure and commercial construction. We operate on a project basis — mobilising the right specialists, equipment and project-management capability for each contract. That keeps our overheads lean, our focus firmly on your site, and lets us take on work of scale and complexity across multiple provinces.

Across both divisions, every project runs through a single accountable partner — reducing coordination risk and simplifying procurement for our clients.
